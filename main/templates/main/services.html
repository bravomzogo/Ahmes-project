Academic Programs

Ordinary Level (Form 1-4)

Comprehensive O-Level education following the Tanzanian national curriculum with additional enrichment programs.

  • Core subjects: Mathematics, English, Kiswahili, Civics, etc.
  • Science and arts streams available
  • ICT integrated across all subjects

Advanced Level - Science

Rigorous A-Level science program preparing students for STEM careers and university education.

  • PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics)
  • CBG (Chemistry, Biology, Geography)
  • Advanced practical laboratory work

Advanced Level - Arts & Business

Comprehensive arts and business program developing critical thinking and analytical skills.

  • HGL (History, Geography, Language)
  • EGM (Economics, Geography, Mathematics)
  • HGE (History, Geography, Economics)
  • Business skills development
