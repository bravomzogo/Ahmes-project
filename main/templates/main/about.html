About AHMES Secondary School

Our History

AHMES secondary school officially opened its doors in 2007, and has been a center of academic excellence for both boys and girls at the secondary level. We offer O – level and A – level education in both science, business and social sciences subjects.

Our Mission

To provide quality mental, physical and spiritual education to the youth, bring the youth together through teamwork in academics, sports and culture regardless of gender, status, race, religion and political ideology.

Our Vision

To be the leading institution in providing holistic education that produces well-rounded individuals who excel academically and contribute positively to society.

Our Core Values

Academic Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in teaching and learning to ensure our students achieve their full potential.

Community

We foster a supportive environment where students, staff, and parents work together as a community.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our interactions and decision-making processes.

Our Achievements

Academic Performance

Consistently ranked in the top 10 schools in Tanzania for NECTA examination results since 2016.

  • 100% pass rate in Form Four examinations for the past 5 years
  • 95% of our Form Six students qualify for university admission
  • 15 national scholarship recipients in the past 3 years
Sports & Extracurriculars

Our students excel beyond the classroom in various competitions:

  • National Science Olympiad winners (2019, 2021)
  • Regional football champions (2020, 2022)
  • Debate competition national finalists (2021, 2023)
  • Music and drama festival regional champions
