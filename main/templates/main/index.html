ABOUT US

Providing quality education since 2007

Ali Hassan Mwinyi Elite Schools (AHMES) is an educational institution that provides care and education for both boys and girls in its campuses. We provide quality mental, physical and spiritual education to the youth, bring the youth together through teamwork in academics, sports and culture regardless of gender, status, race, religion and political ideology.

AHMES manages a nursery and primary school in Kawe campus, located in Dar es Salaam and the Secondary school located in Bagamoyo.

AHMES secondary school officially opened its doors in 2007, and has been a center of academic excellence for both boys and girls at the secondary level. We offer O – level and A – level education in both science, business and social sciences subjects.

Our Campuses

Quality education across multiple locations

Pre and Primary School - Kawe

Our Nursery and Primary school is located at Kawe, Kinondoni district in Dar es Salaam. Our outstanding results in District, Regional and National examinations all standard VII pupils have been scoring A grades for all subjects.

Working Time: Mon - Sat

O - Level Education

Our O-Level Secondary school education provided at Bagamoyo Campus for all Boys and Girls. AHMES excelled as one of the best performing schools in O-Level as published by NECTA in January 2021 in Form Four results.

Working Time: Mon - Sat

Advanced Level Education

A-Level education provided at Bagamoyo Campus for all Boys and Girls and the School has been consistently ranked in Top ten School in NECTA's best performance schools academically since 2016 to date, Our Students score only division one and Two.
